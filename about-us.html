Who we are

Mapping your STEPs a fundamental, crucial movement

We at Olympiadcoach.com believe that learning, assessing and reviewing the knowledge gained and improving wherever necessary, is the best practice for progress and success of a student.

Analysing what is learnt, through graded assessment, helps a student achieve better scores. We help students in mapping every step throughout their academic journey.

STEP stands for Scholastic Testing and Enrichment Programme. Olympiadcoach.com thus influences every step of a student.

Our guiding philosophy has always been to continuously direct all our efforts to enhancing our assessment and learning solution that helps students learn, score and at the same time enjoy the entire process.

We have worked and progressed extensively in the area of content development for Olympiad & Talent Exams preparation, on a regular basis. Our packages help students crack all international, national and regional Science, Maths, English & Cyber Olympiads and Talent exams.

Our leading edge is the result of our domain expertise and instruction design skills. The creation of quality learning solutions is made possible by our highly talented team.
