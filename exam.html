• Your test duration begins as soon as you view the first question.
  • Click the SUBMIT button after selecting your answer option for a question. Only one option can be selected as the answer to a question.
  • You can change the option any number of times before clicking on the SUBMIT button.
  • Your choice of the option will be saved only on clicking the SUBMIT button.
  • Once the SUBMIT button is clicked, the next question will be displayed.
  • Click on 'Next' or 'Previous' buttons to move from one question to another.
  • Your answer for the current question will not be saved automatically if you click on 'Next' or 'Previous' buttons without clicking the SUBMIT button.
  • On the Test Monitor (available on left side on the screen), you can see the change in color from Yellow to Green for the number of questions attempted.
  • You can click on any of your desired question number in the Test Monitor to go to that question directly and change your choice of option.
  • Please make sure to review all the questions before ending the test by clicking on the FINISH button.
  • Once you click on the FINISH button, the test gets completed (even if it is before the duration of 20 minutes) and you will not be able to edit any of the answers.
  • The test session expires on the completion of 20 minutes.
  • You can click on the 'STOP' button to stop the test and continue it later. After you stop a test, you can log out from the portal if you wish to. Once you log in again, you can continue the exam from where you stopped. We highly recommend that you try to complete a test without stopping.
  • Remember, there is no negative marking.
  • Once you complete a test, the result is immediately displayed. It can be accessed from 'My Reports' section at any point of time.
