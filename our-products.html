This critically important ability of reasoning should be seeded and nurtured in the minds of students at their budding age itself. Once the seed of reasoning sprouts in young minds, the way they see the world changes entirely. Life looks like an invigorating puzzle.

Reasoning and logical skills are an integral part of a wide range of subjects such as Math, Science, Design and Technology, etc. These skills are also vital in our day-to-day lives.

Olympiad & Talent Exams Chapter-wise Tests Packages

Mathematics, Science, English:

Each Subject: Rs. 599

All the packages are valid till: 31/3/2024

A student who has discovered the art of learning has begun a journey to success.

Many a time the understanding of fundamental concepts alone does not help students in cracking competitive exams, as most of them are objective in structure. Students need a particular type of training to familiarize themselves with the style of exams they attempt.

The board exams are qualifying in nature and the knowledge gained is tested and assessed at exams that are competitive in nature, conducted in objective type format.

Mapmystep.com has come out with this Olympiad & Talent Exams Preparatory Package, which works as a catalyst in the process of transforming utility of the knowledge gained, for putting it across effectively at competitive exams, thereby enabling students to face the competitive exams with confidence & ease, resulting in high score. This pivotal role that the package plays, makes it utmost essential as a vehicle for students, on their road to success.

Care has been taken to design thought-provoking Application, Skill, Knowledge and Understanding oriented questions. The questions have been reviewed for proper coverage of every aspect and so impart reasoning, logical and analytical skills to build confidence in students.

This package complements the additional preparation needs of students, for the regular board exams.

Package Structure :
  • For every chapter, 5 tests are conducted through 5 unique steps of the assessment process.
  • Each test can be attempted 5 times.
  • Every test is provided with solutions; and with explanations wherever necessary.
  • All tests are backed by interesting and useful Analysis Reports :

Question-wise Analysis : This gives the percentage score of students who answered each and every question correctly or otherwise.
Overall Analysis : This gives the test-wise performance on a single graph (percentile score); an idea of your performance-level compared to all the other test-takers.

This package is very useful for all International, National and Regional −
  • Maths Olympiads
  • Science Olympiads
  • English Olympiads
  • NSTSE
  • NTSE
  • Various Scholarship, Talent Exams and
  • Assessment Exams etc.
The package is available for classes 1 to 10 for the following subjects :
  • Mathematics
  • Science (for lower classes EVS and for higher classes including Physics, Chemistry and Biology)
  • English

 

Test Structure

All questions are of Multiple Choice type with one correct answer. For every chapter, 5 tests are conducted. This is a unique 5 STEP Process.

Step 1:

Presents Test 1 Comprising 15 Basic level Questions

Step 2:

Presents Test 2 Comprising 15 Challenging Questions

Step 3:

Presents Test 3 Comprising 15 Advanced Questions

Step 4:

Presents Test 4 which is a combination of questions answered wrongly in STEPS 1, 2 & 3.

Step 5:

Presents Test 5 Comprising 15 new questions which are a combination of Basic, Challenging and Advanced questions.
